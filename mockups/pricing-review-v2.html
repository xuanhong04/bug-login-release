FAQ

Can I switch plans later?

Yes. Upgrades are immediate and prorated. Downgrades apply on the next billing cycle without deleting your data.

How does yearly discount work?

Yearly billing gives 20% savings while preserving all feature limits of the selected plan.

Where do I manage invoices and payment methods?

All post-subscription operations move to Billing Management, kept separate from this conversion page for cleaner marketing flow.

Ready to activate?
